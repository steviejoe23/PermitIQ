# PermitIQ Sales Outreach Templates

---

## Outreach Playbook

### Who to Target First
1. **Zoning attorneys with 10+ ZBA cases and <90% win rate** -- they have the most to gain from better pre-filing intelligence. Pull from the `/attorneys/leaderboard` endpoint.
2. **Repeat developers** (3+ ZBA filings) -- they already understand the pain and cost of the process.
3. **Developers with recent denials** -- immediate, felt need.

### When to Send
- **Tuesday through Thursday, 8:00-10:00am EST**
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (checked out)
- Attorneys check email early before court/hearings

### Follow-Up Cadence
| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 0 | Initial cold email |
| Day 3 | Follow-up #1 (Template 3) |
| Day 7 | Follow-up #2 -- share a specific insight about their ward or case type |
| Day 14 | Breakup email -- "No worries if not, just wanted to make sure you saw this" |

### What to Track
- **Open rate** (target: 50%+ with personalized subject lines)
- **Reply rate** (target: 15-20%)
- **Demos booked** (target: 10 in first 2 weeks)
- **Referrals generated** per demo

### Goal
**10 demos in 2 weeks.** That means ~60-80 personalized emails across attorneys and developers.
